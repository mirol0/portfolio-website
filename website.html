Building My Personal Portfolio Site

Overview

The aim of this project was to create a functional, responsive personal website that would host my porfolio, highlight my UX/UI design skills demonstrate my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and connect me with potential employers.

Process

In a nutshell, I was responsible for turning my PDF case studies into a responsive web page, while maintaining the pixel perfect visual skills of my UI design background, and infusing it with a great narrative and personal touch. No big deal.

Admittedly, I had the naivety to think exactly that at. I even thought about attempting to build a whole other website simultaneously. My intention was to further distinguish myself from other designers and emphasize my work ethic as I emerge from a career transition period and seek a UX/UI design role. That’s why I told my mentor I wanted to create a portfolio site for the assignment and I would build a second site for my design blog for myself.
